# DoctorLinc & BrainSAIT: Strategic Assets Blueprint ## 1. BrainSAIT Global Healthcare Intelligence Map A living geospatial and relational map of healthcare intelligence across Saudi Arabia and eventually the GCC/Africa. This map serves as the strategic cockpit for BrainSAIT leadership and BOT sales teams. ### Key Layers: - **Geographic Layer:** Zoomable map of KSA with hospital and doctor density. - **Institutional Layer:** Deep dives into specific facilities (e.g., King Faisal Specialist Hospital) showing Sudanese doctor counts and research activity. - **Knowledge & Research Layer:** Real-time feeds from PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, and NIH LISTSERV. - **Technology & Innovation Layer:** Mapping AI health startups and digital maturity indices. - **Opportunity Layer:** Ranked BOT candidates with estimated value and intelligence gaps. --- ## 2. BOS Agent Orchestration Protocol (AOP) A formal protocol for multi-agent collaboration within the BrainSAIT Operating System (BOS). ### Agent Roles: - **Coordinator:** Routes commands and manages workflows. - **Research Agent:** Handles clinical and literature queries. - **Clinical Agent:** Manages medical guidelines and CME logic. - **Policy Agent:** Ensures regulatory compliance (SCFHS, MOH). - **Strategy Agent:** Detects opportunities and scores BOT candidates. - **Content Agent:** Generates reports and certificates. - **Automation Agent:** Deploys pipelines and triggers workflows. ### Communication Patterns: - Request-Response - Chain of Responsibility - Parallel Fan-Out - Human-in-the-Loop --- ## 3. DoctorLinc Integration Layer with BOS & BOT Connects the physician-centric DoctorLinc platform to the enterprise-level BrainSAIT ecosystem. ### Integration Pillars: - **Identity & Credential Federation:** Universal SCFHS key and shared verification badges. - **CPD & Learning Sync:** BOS research feeds become DoctorLinc CME content. - **BOT Project Talent & Recruitment:** Surfacing paid advisory roles to verified doctors. ### Technical Touchpoints: - **API Gateway:** Bi-directional communication between BOS and DoctorLinc. - **Event Webhooks:** Real-time triggers for verification and CME completion. - **Shared Data Views:** Lead scoring and hospital coverage metrics. - **Sidecar Deployment:** On-premise containers for data localization (SDAIA compliance).
