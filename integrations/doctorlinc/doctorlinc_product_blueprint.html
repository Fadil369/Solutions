# DoctorLinc: Professional Network for Sudanese Doctors in KSA ## Core Concept A niche professional network focused on **trust + density**, specifically designed for the Sudanese medical diaspora in Saudi Arabia. ## Killer Features 1. **Verify Me in 2 Minutes:** Automated SCFHS ID verification with shareable badges. 2. **Who's Who at My Hospital:** Hyper-local networking within specific facilities. 3. **Locum Shift Swaps:** High-utility marketplace for shift coverage. 4. **Linc Score:** Peer-to-peer reputation and endorsements. 5. **SCFHS CME Tracker:** Administrative tool for credit management. 6. **Referral Network:** Warm intros for specialized patient care. 7. **Patient Request Feed:** Income-generating symptom posts. ## Technical Architecture - **Stack:** Node.js, PostgreSQL (PostGIS), Supabase/Auth0, AWS S3. - **Verification Levels:** - L1: Community Verified (License upload) - L2: Document Verified (Admin check) - L3: Authority Verified (SCFHS API/Digital ID check) ## Design System Strategy - **Colors:** Medical Teal (#008080), Trust Blue (#1E3A8A), White. - **Typography:** Inter. - **Device:** Desktop (as requested), but designed with mobile-first utility principles. ## Initial Screen List 1. **Dashboard:** Overview of hospital activity, open shifts, and pending referrals. 2. **Doctor Profile:** Showcasing verification levels, Linc Score, and professional history. 3. **Shift Swap Feed:** List of available shifts with quick-claim functionality. 4. **Onboarding/Verification:** The step-by-step process to reach L3 verification.
